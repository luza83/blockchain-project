Traceability



True story is a tool that allows producers to keep records of all the stages of their processes, and gives an overview of these processes to the final consumer.


- Register a transaction: fill up the form fields and click on create data to verify your in formation is correct.

* Organization name: your organization name.
* Source: name of the firma or source where the product came from.
* Amount in: how many kg where received.
* Destination: name of the firma your product was sold to.
* Amount out: how many kg where sold

Enter address and key to save in the block chain.

- Transactions overview: Enter address to retrieve overview.
